About Us

Na-Rah is an online personal shopping service, founded in 2019 by Natalie Dean and Farah Bouguerra. Our aim is to provide you with a top quality personal shopping service from the comfort of your own home. Na-Rah appreciate how busy life can be, so to ensure you are looking and feeling your best, we are here to help you save time and look amazing!! We have a fantastic team with 20 years of experience in the field ready to find the perfect outfit for you to suit any occasion. We are able to provide a 24 hour service, for those last minute emergencies, delivered straight to your home or work address. The founders of Na-Rah believe you should never miss out on looking your best for any occasion, we welcome you to Na-Rah and promise you that you will always look your best from this day forward.

Our Services

Wardrobe Assessment

Did you know that women wear only 20% of their wardrobe? Because we know that having a closet full of clothes and “having nothing to wear” is a common problem, we want you to clear out or even rediscover the remaining 80% and to do so, we offer home-visits to discover your wardrobe, figure out what needs to stay and what needs to be cleared out. We will make sure to get rid of all the unflattering items that don’t fit you anymore and update the items you need to feel fabulous!

Shopping Trips

Our personal shopping service in which we provide, is with you in mind. We shop to suit your shape, your preferences, your style, brand, fabric, size/fit and of course occasion/event. What’s important to you, is important to us here at Na-Rah. When you sign up you will be asked to complete a series of questions in order for us to help select the finest items of clothing to your acquired taste. You can change your preferences at any point, and out stylist’s will automatically be updated. When you receive your hand-picked items, you will also receive some styling cards, with ideas on how to pair the item with others and our stylists top tips!

Complete Styling Package

Our complete styling packages include wardrobe assessment and shopping sessions , but the key to this service is to be open-minded ! At Na-Rah we believe that it important to be open-minded to changes. We may find you an item which may differ from your preferences, but our trusted stylists have selected that item with you in mind. It may be something different to what you had in mind, but we encourage you to always try something new. If you really feel that a piece isn’t for you, that is no problem at all, you can just send it straight back to us and we can send you an alternative item.

Last Minute Panic

You have a last minute important plan and do not have anything to wear? Don't panic! With our emergency plan service, we always have your back , no matter the situation! Na-Rah offers you the possibility to slay at any time for any occasion with our 24hours assistance.
